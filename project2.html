Create Task

For part of the AP Computer Science princilples course, I was tasked with completing a Create Performance Task, something that involved developing a computer program of my choice. In addition to that, as part of the AP exam I had to answer a series of questions in order to explain how my code works, how it handles input/output, how I used algorithms, and various other aspects.

Prior to settling on my final project idea, I went through numerous concepts. I really struggled with overthinking during the entire process, finding it difficult to come up with an idea that felt original enough to not get labeled as copying off of another create task submission, but not so complex that I would not be able to properly explain it during the AP exam. The idea for a basic water tracker came to me after I forgot my water bottle ay home one school day and realized how easy it is to loose track of hydration. As I refined the scope and features of my code, I developed a proper understanding of how it worked, enough so that I felt very confident in my ability to explain it during the Create Task questions.

Here is a link to my completed project repository: https://github.com/luck4sc/create-task-practice-1
